2. The quality of an individual's response to membership in a community

Acts of Citizenship

Pillar Definition Importance
Honesty a. fairness and straightforwardness of conduct
b. adherence to the facts		 Honesty is an important aspect of citizenship because when someone is honest people tend to have trust for that person. Having trust for one another allows for people to work together as a society.
Compassion sympathetic consciousness of others' distress
together with a desire to alleviate it		 Compassion is necessary because it leads people to help one another. Without compassion people would only think of themselves and that is not what being a citizen is.
Respect a. an act of giving particular attention
b. high or special regard		 Respect is a crucial aspect of citizenship, when people have respect for one another they are able to share ideas and build things together.
Responsibility moral, legal, or mental accountability Responsibility is an important aspect of citizenship because when everybody does their part societies run smoothly.
Courage mental or moral strength to persevere, and
withstand danger, fear, or difficulty		 Courage is important to have as a citizen. If you are being treated unfairly you must hve the courage to speak up against those keeping you down and make a change
Cooperation association of persons for common benefit Cooperation is critical to being a good citizen because if you are not willing to work with others then a society cannot grow.
