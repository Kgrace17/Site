Image Formats on the Web

PNG
There are two types of PNGs, PNG-8 and PNG-24. A PNG-8 file has
has simliar quality to GIFs and is also limited to a maximum of 256 colors.
PNG-8 is better for smaller files however, PNG-24 can give greater quality. A
downside to PNGs is that file sizes are comparable larger to JPEGs and GIFs.
GIF
GIFs have a maximum availibility of 256 colors. This image format is not ideal
if you want your images to have lots of detailed colorsand transitions however,
simple images with blocks of color will look good as a GIF
JPEG
JPEG images can support up to 16 million colors and are good for detailed images
with gradients.
